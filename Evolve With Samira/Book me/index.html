FAQ

Questions, answered.

How does the free session work? +
The free 30-minute clarity session is a no-obligation call where we explore your current situation, what you want to change, and whether Samira is the right coach for you. There is zero pressure to commit to anything.
Are sessions online or in person? +
All sessions are conducted online via video call (Zoom or Google Meet), meaning Samira can work with clients worldwide, regardless of location. 🌍
What areas do you specialise in? +
Samira specialises in confidence building, career transition, executive leadership, personal purpose, and overcoming rejection. Every coaching relationship is tailored to your unique situation.
How quickly will I see results? +
Many clients report feeling a shift in mindset after just the first session! Deeper transformation typically unfolds over 4–6 weeks of consistent coaching and action-taking between sessions. ✨
Is everything confidential? +
Yes — completely. All sessions are fully confidential. What is shared in coaching stays in coaching. A coaching agreement is signed at the start of every engagement. 🔒
Can I change my package after starting? +
Absolutely! If you find you need more depth or a different focus, we can adjust your package. Samira's priority is your progress and happiness, not a rigid structure.
